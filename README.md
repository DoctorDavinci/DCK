DCK - Davinci's Combat Kit

Adds 22 camouflage patterns, 19 solid colors and 1 Waldo to Stock, Aviator Arsenal. BD Armory Continued, BDMk22 Cockpits, SM_AFVs, Mk2 Expansion, QuizTech, KAX, Airplane Plus, BAD T Props; ALG, AoA Tech, RvB, SXT and Procedural Parts as well as most of SMI, SMA and the rest of Spannermonkey(smce)'s mods having built in support for DCK

Adds DCK Wheels which gives you a selection of tires and rims for your stock rover wheels

DCK also contains Transparent/Invisible Aircraft Armor (FAR compatibility MM patch included for those who use DCK Aircraft Armor with FAR) to protect your shiny new paintjob ... Must Have DCK_FutureTech installed in order to utilize them
